Frequently Asked Questions

How does SaaS Down Radar check service status?

We fetch real-time data from public status pages using the Atlassian Statuspage API and other public APIs. Most major SaaS services publish their status through these standardized APIs. Status is checked automatically every 5 minutes and cached locally for fast page loads.

Is SaaS Down Radar free?

Yes, SaaS Down Radar is completely free to use. No account or registration is required. Just open the page and see the current status of all monitored services.

How many services do you monitor?

We currently monitor 20+ major SaaS services across categories including Developer Tools, Cloud Providers, Communication, AI Services, Productivity, Payments, and Monitoring. We plan to add more services over time.

What do the status colors mean?

  • Green (Operational) - All systems are working normally
  • Yellow (Degraded) - Service is experiencing performance issues
  • Orange (Partial Outage) - Some components are down
  • Red (Major Outage) - Service is significantly impacted or down
  • Gray (Unknown) - Unable to determine status

How do I search for a specific service?

Use the search bar at the top of the dashboard. You can type the service name (e.g., "ChatGPT", "GitHub", "Slack") or even "Is GitHub down?" and the dashboard will filter to show matching services. You can also press the / key to quickly focus the search bar.

What is the uptime percentage?

The uptime percentage shown on each service card is an estimated 30-day uptime based on the current status and recent incident history. It provides a general sense of service reliability.

Can I see individual components for a service?

Yes! Click on any service card to expand it and see the status of individual components (e.g., API, Web App, CDN, etc.). You'll also find a link to the official status page for more details.

Why does a service show "Unknown" status?

A service may show "Unknown" if we were unable to reach its status page due to network issues, rate limiting, or if the service's status page API is temporarily unavailable. The status will update on the next refresh cycle.

Is the data real-time?

Status data is refreshed every 5 minutes. The "Last checked" timestamp on each card shows exactly when the data was last updated. You can see the countdown timer in the header for the next refresh.
