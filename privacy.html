Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

SaaS Down Radar collects minimal data to improve the service. We collect:

  • Visit data: Timestamp, page visited, browser language, timezone
  • Search queries: What services you search for (anonymized)
  • User agent: Browser type and version
  • Local storage: Cached status data and visit counter stored in your browser

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • No IP addresses are stored
  • No account registration is required
  • No cookies are used for tracking (only localStorage for caching)
  • No personally identifiable information (PII)

3. How We Use Information

The data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which services are most frequently searched
  • Improve the dashboard based on usage patterns
  • Display visitor statistics (anonymized counts)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For advertising. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out at Google Ads Settings.
  • Adsterra: For advertising. Adsterra may use tracking technologies. See their privacy policy for details.
  • Google Sheets: For anonymous visit logging via webhooks.
  • Vercel: For hosting. Vercel may collect access logs.
  • Atlassian Statuspage APIs: For fetching service status data.

5. Data Storage

Visit data is stored in Google Sheets, accessible only to the site administrator. Local cache data is stored in your browser's localStorage and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR and similar data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection (by disabling JavaScript)
  • Clear local storage data through your browser settings

7. Children's Privacy

SaaS Down Radar does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
