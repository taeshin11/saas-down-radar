2. Description of Service

SaaS Down Radar is a free, browser-based dashboard that monitors and displays the publicly reported operational status of third-party software-as-a-service (SaaS) platforms. The Service aggregates status data from official public status pages and APIs maintained by the respective service providers. The dashboard presents this data in a unified, user-friendly interface with color-coded status indicators, service search and filtering, incident history, and estimated uptime information.

The Service is provided free of charge and requires no user registration, subscription, or payment of any kind. We may display advertisements on the Service as our primary means of sustaining operations.

3. Data Accuracy Disclaimer

The status information displayed on SaaS Down Radar is provided on a best-effort basis. Specifically, you should be aware of the following important limitations:

  • Status data is sourced from third parties. We do not independently verify or test service availability. The status we display reflects what each service's official status page reports — if a vendor delays updating their status page during an incident (which is common), our dashboard will reflect that delay.
  • Polling intervals introduce latency. Status data is refreshed every five minutes. In rapidly evolving incidents, actual service conditions may differ from what is currently displayed.
  • Status may not reflect all users. A service may report "Operational" even when a subset of users or geographic regions is experiencing problems. Vendor status pages reflect the vendor's assessment of their own infrastructure, which may not match every user's individual experience.
  • Data is not guaranteed to be complete or error-free. Temporary API failures, rate limiting, or network issues may cause status data for specific services to be unavailable or stale at any given time.
  • Uptime percentages are estimates. The uptime percentages shown on service cards are calculated estimates based on available status data and should not be used as contractual uptime metrics.

SaaS Down Radar is intended as a convenient reference tool, not as a definitive or guaranteed source of service availability information. For critical decisions, always consult the service's official status page directly.
