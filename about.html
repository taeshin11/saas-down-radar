About SaaS Down Radar

SaaS Down Radar is a free, real-time dashboard that monitors the status of 20+ major SaaS services that developers, remote workers, and businesses rely on every day.

What We Monitor

We track services across multiple categories including Developer Tools (GitHub, Vercel, Netlify, npm), Cloud Providers (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, Cloudflare), Communication (Slack, Discord, Zoom), AI Services (ChatGPT/OpenAI), Productivity (Notion, Figma), Payments (Stripe), and Monitoring (Datadog, Twilio).

How It Works

SaaS Down Radar fetches real-time status data from public status pages, primarily using the Atlassian Statuspage API. The dashboard displays color-coded status indicators (green for operational, yellow for degraded, orange for partial outage, red for major outage) with last-checked timestamps and estimated uptime percentages.

  • Status checks run automatically every 5 minutes
  • Data is cached locally for fast page loads
  • Click any service card to see individual component status
  • Use the search bar to quickly check if a specific service is down
  • Recent incidents from all monitored services are displayed in a unified feed

Why We Built This

When a service you depend on goes down, the first thing most people do is search "Is X down?" We built SaaS Down Radar to be the fastest, most comprehensive answer to that question. Instead of checking individual status pages, you can see the health of all your critical services at a glance.

Our Principles

  • Free forever - No accounts, no subscriptions, no paywalls
  • Privacy-first - We collect minimal data and never track you personally
  • Transparent - We show exactly when data was last checked and link to official status pages
  • Fast - Built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS for maximum performance
  • Accessible - Designed for all users with semantic HTML and ARIA labels
